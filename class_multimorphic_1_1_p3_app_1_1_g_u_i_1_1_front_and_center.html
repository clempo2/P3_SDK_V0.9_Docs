Detailed Description

"The lens hugger" Add this script to a GameObject to make it always appear a certain distance in front of the camera, facing the camera. The GameObject will be constantly scaled so that width and height in its own coordinate space will fit into the field of view. It's best to add this to an empty GameObject and then add child objects (those which are to appear within the frame). The frameWidth and frameHeight should usually be set to have the same aspect ratio as the screen, but this is neither mandaatory nor enforced. Works with orthographic and perspective camera projections.
