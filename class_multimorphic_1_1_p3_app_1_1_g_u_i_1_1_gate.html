Detailed Description

A gate through the passage of the ball avatar can be detected. Consists of two colliders: an entry collider and an exit collider. Passage is identified by the exit collider being hit shortly after the entry collider. Can be set to detect passage in a single direction or both directions. Also has "post" colliders, which, if hit, negate the passage.
